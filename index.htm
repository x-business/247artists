FAQ

What is 24/7 Artists and what does it offer?
24/7 Artists is a global community of creative professionals and enthusiasts that offers creative-led courses, world-class creator tools, and a platform to connect with other artists in the music industry.
Who can join the 24/7 Artists community?
Anyone who is passionate about music and the arts can join the 24/7 Artists community.
How do I join the 24/7 Artists community?
You can join the 24/7 Artists community by signing up for a membership on the website.
Are there any membership fees to join the community?
Yes, there is a membership fee to join the 24/7 Artists community. The fee varies depending on the membership plan you choose. There is also a free version.
How do I access the creative-led courses on the website?
You can access the creative-led courses by logging into your account and browsing the course catalog.
What types of courses are available on 24/7 Artists?
24/7 Artists offers a wide variety of courses on topics such as music business, music production, songwriting, vocal technique, instrument mastery, and personal development.
How do I get started with the creator tools available on the website?
You can get started with the CreatorSPC tools by logging into your account and accessing the tools section of the website.
Can I sell my art or music on the 24/7 Artists platform?
No, we do not have a selling platform on our platform at this time.
How can I connect with other artists in the community?
You can connect with other artists in the community by joining groups, attending events, and collaborating on projects.
What types of events does 24/7 Artists organize?
24/7 Artists organizes events such as live concerts, workshops, and masterclasses.
How can I participate in the events organized by 24/7 Artists?
You can participate in the events organized by 24/7 Artists by registering on the website or attending in-person if the event is held locally.
Can I submit my own event to be featured on the website?
No, but you can post your events, music, and other announcements in our community channel “Plug Yourself.”
How can I stay updated with the latest news and updates from 24/7 Artists?
You can stay updated with the latest news and updates by subscribing to the 24/7 Artists newsletter, following their social media accounts, or checking the website regularly.
What are the benefits of joining the 24/7 Artists community?
The benefits of joining the 24/7 Artists community include access to creative-led courses, creator tools, a platform to connect with other artists, and opportunities for collaboration and networking.
How can I contribute to the community as a member?
You can contribute to the community by sharing your knowledge and expertise, participating in events and projects, and supporting other members.
Is there a support team available to assist me with any issues or questions?
Yes, there is a support team available to assist you with any issues or questions you may have. You can contact them through the website or email [email protected]
Can I access 24/7 Artists on my mobile device?
Yes, our website, community, and courses are all built with mobile-friendly designs.
How do I cancel my membership with 24/7 Artists?
You can cancel your membership through the settings page on your CreatorSPC dashboard.
How can I provide feedback or suggestions to the 24/7 Artists team?
You can provide feedback in our “Ideas & Feedback” community channel, or by emailing [email protected]

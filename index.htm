FAQ

What is tape.pink, and how does music tokenization work?
tape.pink is a Web3-native platform where music becomes a digital asset. Artists can tokenize songs, assign royalty splits, and let fans invest and trade directly — no middlemen, just smart contracts.
Who can launch a token on tape.pink?
Any artist, producer, or rights-holder can mint a music token — whether you're independent, signed, or just starting out.
What does a "music token" actually represent?
It can represent a share of royalties, access to exclusive content, or co-ownership of a track. You define the value of your token — we handle the blockchain backend.
Is tape.pink free to use?
Yes! Anyone can sign up and explore. Advanced features like custom token contracts, analytics, and DAO tools are included in CreatorSPC+ (starting at 0.01 ETH/month).
What blockchain does tape.pink run on?
tape.pink currently uses Ethereum and Layer 2 chains (Polygon, Arbitrum) to keep fees low and transactions fast. More chains coming soon.
Do I need crypto to use tape.pink?
Not to get started. We make onboarding easy with email wallets and fiat payments. If you want to trade or withdraw earnings, you'll need a wallet like MetaMask.
What's the benefit of fan ownership?
Fans become backers, not just listeners. They get exclusive access, early drops, and in some cases, a share of revenue. It's crowdfunding meets crypto — built for music.
Can I launch an entire album as tokens?
Absolutely. You can tokenize singles, EPs, or full-length albums. You can even assign different rights or experiences to each track.
How does revenue splitting work?
You can set up automatic, on-chain splits when you mint your token. Revenue goes straight to collaborators' wallets based on the percentages you define.
Can fans resell music tokens?
Yes. Music tokens can be traded on supported marketplaces, just like NFTs. You can also set royalty fees for every resale.
How can I participate in tape.pink drops or token launch events?
You can join our community Discord or subscribe to the newsletter to get notified of upcoming drops, token launches, and live sessions. Early supporters often get whitelist access or perks.
Can I host my own token drop or listening event through tape.pink?
Yes — you can schedule public or token-gated releases, organize virtual launch events, or coordinate with the community for collab drops. All from your CreatorSPC dashboard.
Can I access tape.pink on my mobile device?
Yes, our website, community, and courses are all built with mobile-friendly designs.
How do I cancel or pause my CreatorSPC+ membership?
You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your CreatorSPC dashboard under "Billing & Access." We'll be here if you ever want to rejoin the movement.
